Observed product signals:

- AllAi is positioned as a unified workflow for AI images, video, text, voice, templates, and social publishing.
- The landing page references 28 AI models, 6 content formats, and publishing to 3 social networks.
- Generator formats include Photo, Video, Upscale, Avatars, and Motion.
- Video model names observed include Kling, Seedance, Grok Video, Gemini Omni, Wan, Hailuo, Veo, Runway, and Sora.
- Pricing is coin-based; coins are presented as non-expiring packages.
- Pricing/generator signals include Nano Banana, Nano Banana Pro, Kling, Kling 3.0, Seedream, Seedance, Grok Image, Topaz Upscale, Flux, Flux 2 Pro/Flex, Wan 2.7 Image, Veo 3.1, Runway, and Gemini Image.
- Studio focuses on social content workflows for Pinterest, YouTube, TikTok, and multi-post flows.

Important product assumption:

- The mobile app should not hardcode the final model list. Models, templates, capabilities, prices, availability, and supported inputs must come from backend catalog configuration.
